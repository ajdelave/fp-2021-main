Overview of all 2021 Formula 1 Teams

Feel free to hover or tab over any of the teams for further information

Mercedes AMG Petronas

The Powerhouse

Mercedes has consistently been the best team on the grid over the last decade. They have won 7 straight constructors championships under the guidance of racing director Toto Wolff. If you appreciate meticulous excellence, Mercedes is the team for you.

Red Bull Racing

The Raging Bulls

It seems to strike most people as odd that Red Bull participates in Formula 1. However since their first race in 1997, they've won 4 constructors championships and are currently Mercedes closeset rival. If you appreciate the steady pursuit, Red Bull is the team for you.

McLaren Racing

A Storied History

McLaren is one of the most iconic names in all of motorsport. Winning 8 constrcutors championships since their inception, their are one of the most storied teams on track. Despite some setbacks in the last five years, they are rising towards dominance again. If you appreciate history, McLaren is the team for you.

Aston Martin

The Smooth Criminal

Aston Martin is the most controversial yet sleek team on the grid. The notorious British racing green color is idenitfiable anywhere. However, last year they were penalized for cheating by "copying" the Mercedes car. If you'll do whatever it takes to win, Aston Martin is the team for you.

Alpine

Big Promises

Alpine is a rebrand from the previous Renault F1 team. Having made slow but steady progress over that last few seasons, Alpine is poised to close the gap to the frontrunners. If you can believe in a promise and wait a few years, than Apline is the team for you.

Scuderia Ferrari

The Brand

Ferrari has been with Formula 1 since its inception. They are the largest, loudest, and most passionate team on the grid. They've struggled in recent years, however their fanbase of "Scuderia" are still unlike any other. If you want to be apart of the biggest brand, Ferrari is the team for you.

Alpha Tauri

The Showcase

Alpha Tauri is a unique team in Formula 1, as they act almost as a "B" team to Red Bull. It is common for drivers to be promoted from Alpha Tauri to Red Bull. Their cars are almost always slower due to lack of funding however. Regardless, if you enjoy watching young drivers showcase their talent, Alpha Tauri is the team for you.

Alfa Romeo Racing

Consistency is Key

Alfa Romeo is an interesting team unfortunatley plagued by lack of funding. Without the budget to compete against larger teams, Alfa has struggled to compete outside of the mid-field. However, they have excellent liveries for their cars and have likeable drivers. If you appreciate beauty on a car, Alfa Romeo is the team for you.

HAAS

American Grit

HAAS has the distinction of being the only American owned and operated team on the Formula 1 grid. Despite similar budget constraints that constrain smaller teams, HAAS consistently punches above their weightclass, just as they do in Nascar. If you want to root for an American team, than HAAS is the team for you.

Williams

A Slow Decline

Williams was the last privatley operated team on the grid. They were lead by the Williams family and won numerous championships prior to 2000. Lack of funding however has plauged the team and caused them to drop to last in the constrcutor. If you appreciate the grind and respect family traditions, Williams is the team for you.
